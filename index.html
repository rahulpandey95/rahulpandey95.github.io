About

Rahul Pandey is a PhD candidate at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi. His research focuses on contemporary Chinese politics and the governance structures of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). He writes on India’s domestic politics, financial markets, and strategic affairs, and contributes to policy research at think tanks.
