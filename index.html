Rahul Pandey

I am Rahul Pandey, a PhD Candidate at the Centre for East Asian Studies (China Division), School of International Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University. My research focuses on the domestic politics of China, particularly the Chinese Communist Party (CCP), and its implications for governance, elite politics, and ideology.

I regularly write columns and commentaries on Chinese domestic politics, China–US relations, military technology, financial markets, and great power competition for national and international outlets and think tanks.

  • MPhil, Centre for East Asian Studies (China Division), School of International Studies, JNU
  • MA Political Science (Honours), University of Delhi
