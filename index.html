Rahul Pandey

PhD Candidate, Centre for East Asian Studies (China Division), School of International Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University

Rahul Pandey is a PhD Candidate at the Centre for East Asian Studies (China Division), School of International Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University. He completed his Bachelor’s and Master’s in Political Science (Honours) from the University of Delhi and an MPhil from the Chinese Division, Centre for East Asian Studies, School of International Studies, JNU.

His primary research examines the domestic politics of China, with a particular focus on the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). His broader interests include authoritarian governance, political economy, China–US relations, military technology, and great power competition.

He writes regularly for national and international platforms, contributing commentary, columns, and policy analysis to a range of outlets and think tanks.
