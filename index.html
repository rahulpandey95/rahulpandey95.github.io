About

Rahul Pandey is a PhD candidate at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), New Delhi. His research focuses on contemporary Chinese politics and the evolving governance structures of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). His broader interests include authoritarian political systems, global political economy, comparative politics, and the political economy of markets. Rahul writes for national and international audiences and contributes to policy research at think tanks.
