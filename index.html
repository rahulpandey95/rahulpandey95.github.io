About

Rahul Pandey is a PhD Candidate at the Centre for East Asian Studies (China Division), School of International Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University. He completed his Bachelor’s and Master’s in Political Science (Honours) from the University of Delhi and an MPhil from the Chinese Division in the Centre for East Asian Studies, School of International Studies, JNU.

His research focuses on the domestic politics of China, particularly the Chinese Communist Party (CCP). He writes regularly on Chinese domestic politics, China–US relations, military technology, and great power competition for national and international outlets and think tanks.
